## 🧠 Smart Study Companion (AI + Analytics)

**Tagline:** *“Notion + Duolingo + AI Tutor”*

### 📌 Project Description

**Smart Study Companion** is a full-stack web application that acts as a **personal AI-powered study coach** for students.
It helps students **plan their studies, understand topics using AI, practice with auto-generated quizzes, and track their performance using analytics**.

Instead of studying blindly, students get **data-driven guidance** on:

* What to study
* How much to study
* Which topics they are weak in
* How ready they are for exams

The system uses **AI for explanations and quiz generation** and **analytics to adapt difficulty, suggest weak areas, and predict exam readiness**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Help students **organize their syllabus into manageable tasks**
* Provide **AI-based topic explanations** in simple language
* Generate **automatic quizzes** for practice
* Track **study time and performance**
* Visualize progress using **heatmaps and analytics**
* Adapt content difficulty based on performance
* Predict **exam readiness level**

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

* ✅ **Timetable & Task Planner** – Break syllabus into daily goals
* ⏱️ **Study Time Tracking** – Track hours per topic and subject
* 🤖 **AI Tutor** – Get topic-wise explanations and examples
* 📝 **Auto Quiz Generator** – Practice MCQs and questions
* 📊 **Performance Analytics** – See weak/strong topics via graphs & heatmaps
* 🧠 **Adaptive Learning** – AI adjusts difficulty based on performance
* 📈 **Exam Readiness Predictor** – Shows how prepared the student is
* 🏆 **Leaderboard** – Optional social motivation among friends

---

## 🧩 How It Works (In Simple Flow)

```
Student sets syllabus
      ↓

      ↓

      ↓
Takes AI-generated quiz
      ↓

      ↓

      ↓

      ↓
Exam readiness score updated
```
